Pave a path for your imagination and knowledge.

Welcome to Tales and Tomes, your sanctuary for the written word, and passport to faraway lands, mystical realms, and the depths of the human mind. Located at the heart of our vibrant community, Tales and Tomes isn't just a bookstore — it's a haven for book lovers, a meeting place for thinkers, and a launchpad for dreamers. Imagine a place where the air is tinged with the scent of fresh ink and aging parchment, where the very walls seem to whisper stories from the annals of history and the boundless realms of fantasy. That's Tales and Tomes for you. Here, you're not just a customer; you're part of a family bonded by an unquenchable thirst for knowledge and imagination. Our carefully curated collection spans genres, philosophies, and languages, ensuring that there's a tale for every taste and a tome for every questing mind. From bestselling thrillers to indie poetry, educational texts to graphic novels, you’ll always find something that speaks to you. Come experience the magic and the endless possibilities that await within our pages. Because in a world of fleeting pleasures, a book is a faithful companion.

Our Mission

To provide books without harming the enviroment. We create our books with alchemy and hemp to save trees that normal book stores would otherwise destroy. We aim to make a change in the book industry, and save our planet through unconventional and sustainable means.
